Chutney

Chutney is a bookmarklet that you can use to find out how companies you are doing business are wielding political influence through lobbying and campaign contributions.

The bookmarklet: → Chutney ← (drag this to your bookmarks bar, or right-click and "bookmark this")

Next, just go to any web page that has bank transactions (your banking site, credit card site, etc.) and click on the bookmarklet. Chutney will show you the influence data for all the companies it can identify.

Privacy

To ensure that we respect your privacy, we are very careful not to store any information about your bank transactions that could possibly be personally identifiable. In order to function, Chutney needs to transmit names that it has pulled out of your bank transactions to our server to match them against corporations, but nothing else (no dates, addresses, amounts, or anything) is sent to us. The names are cleaned to ensure that there is nothing sensitive or personally identifiable in them. All connections to the Chutney server are made using industry-standard SSL – the same technology that your banking site uses to ensure the privacy of your data.
